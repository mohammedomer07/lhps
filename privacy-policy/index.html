Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 22/06/2026

Welcome to Leading High School ("School", "we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our students, parents, guardians, staff, visitors, and users of our website and services. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard personal information.

By accessing our website or using our services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.


Information We Collect

We may collect student information including full name, date of birth, gender, residential address, photographs and videos, academic records and examination results, attendance records, health and emergency information, and extracurricular activity records.

We may collect parent and guardian information including name, contact numbers, email addresses, residential address, emergency contact information, and billing and payment details.

We may collect staff and employee information including identification details, educational qualifications, employment records, payroll and statutory information, and attendance and performance records.

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect IP address, browser information, device information, operating system, date and time of access, pages visited, cookies, and analytical information.


Purpose of Information Collection

The information collected is used for student admissions and enrollment, maintaining academic and administrative records, communicating with parents and guardians, conducting examinations and evaluations, managing school events and activities, processing fee payments, ensuring student safety and security, improving our educational services and website, and complying with legal obligations.


Photographs and Media

Leading High School may capture photographs, videos, and recordings during annual functions, school events, sports competitions, educational activities, classroom projects, and celebrations. Such materials may be used on the school website, social media platforms, brochures, newsletters, publications, and other educational or promotional materials. Parents or guardians may contact the school administration regarding concerns relating to media usage.


Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytical tools to improve website functionality, enhance user experience, analyze traffic, store user preferences, and maintain website security. Users may disable cookies through their browser settings, although certain features may not function properly.


Sharing of Information

Leading High School does not sell personal information. Information may be shared with educational boards, regulatory authorities, government agencies, trusted service providers involved in hosting, cloud storage, communication services, technical support, and payment processing. Information may also be disclosed when required by law or in circumstances necessary to protect the safety and well-being of students, staff, and others.


Data Security

We employ reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, misuse, alteration, loss, and destruction. While we strive to ensure the security of information, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is completely secure.


Data Retention

Information is retained for as long as necessary to maintain academic and administrative records, fulfill educational purposes, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce school policies. Information may subsequently be securely deleted or anonymized.


Children's Privacy

Protecting children's privacy is of utmost importance. Student information is collected solely for legitimate educational, administrative, and safety purposes. Parents and guardians may contact the school administration regarding any concerns related to their child's personal information.


Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites and services. Leading High School is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of external websites. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of such websites before providing personal information.


Rights of Parents and Users

Subject to applicable laws, parents, guardians, and users may request access to their personal information, request correction of inaccurate records, update information, raise concerns regarding data processing activities, and request deletion of information where legally permissible.


Communication

Leading High School may communicate with students, parents, and guardians through telephone calls, SMS, WhatsApp, email, notices, circulars, website announcements, and social media platforms. Such communications are intended for educational, administrative, and emergency purposes.


Online Payments

Online payments may be processed through secure third-party payment gateways. Leading High School does not store complete debit card, credit card, or banking credentials on its servers.


Compliance with Applicable Laws

Leading High School endeavors to comply with applicable Indian laws and regulations relating to education, privacy, and information security.



Website Design and Intellectual Property Rights

The official website of Leading High School has been designed, developed, optimized, and deployed by Mr. Mohammed Omer, Founder of SphereRoot. All website source code, custom development, layouts, user interface designs, graphics, optimizations, deployment configurations, and associated digital assets are protected under applicable copyright and intellectual property laws.

All web design, development, optimization, and deployment rights are reserved by SphereRoot. Unauthorized copying, reproduction, redistribution, modification, reverse engineering, or commercial use of any part of the website without prior written permission from Mr. Mohammed Omer or SphereRoot is strictly prohibited.


Changes to This Privacy Policy

Leading High School reserves the right to amend or update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes shall become effective immediately upon publication on the website. Continued use of our website and services after such changes constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
