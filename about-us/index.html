Late Dr. Gulam Jeelani MA, B.Ed (Osm), RMP Founder & Chief Promoter Leading Group

Late Dr. Gulam Jeelani MA, B.Ed (Osm), RMP Founder & Chief Promoter Leading Group

01

About Our Founder: He started Leading High School in the year 1972 with a vision and mission of education for all — irrespective of religion, caste, creed, and financial status. His dream was to educate every child. He was committed to both Islamic and modern education. Alhamdulillah, today, more than 20,000 students have studied in this prestigious institution, and they are shining in different parts of the world with meritorious accomplishments in their respective fields.

02

His Legacy Lives On: He passed away on 3rd July 2020 (Friday) to meet the Creator. Now, his legacy is carried forward by his son Dr. Muhammad Ahmed. We pray Allah (SWT) to have mercy and grant Jannat ul-Firdous to our beloved founder, and make Leading High School one of the top institutions of the country.
