Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Get answers to most asked questions.

What is Leading High School?

Leading High School is an innovative educational institution dedicated to fostering critical thinking, creativity, and a passion for learning among students.

What grades does Leading High School serve?

We offer education from Nursery to Tenth Grade.

What curriculum does Leading High School follow?

Our curriculum integrates core subjects with research-based methodologies to enhance student engagement and understanding

Are there extracurricular activities?

Yes, we provide a variety of extracurricular activities including sports, arts, clubs, and community service opportunities.

How can I enroll my child?

Enrollment information can be found on our website.

What are the school hours?

Our regular school hours are from 8:30 AM to 3:30 PM, on Monday through Friday.
